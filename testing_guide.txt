Testing:

to test bgp, you can use "show bgp summary"
to test IS-IS , you can use "show isis neighbor"

test eBGP: 
	show bgp vrf B summary 
	show bgp vrf A summary 
	

on ceA and ceB, you can test with pings:	
ping6 fc00:1a::1                 # PE1 eth1
ping6 fc00:1b::2                 # PE2 eth1


To test srv6 locator on PE1 and PE2:
show segment-routing srv6 locator
show segment-routing srv6 sid
